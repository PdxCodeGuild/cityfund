Invest in Index Funds

Invest for ownership in CityFund Index Funds. Funds each have equity stakes in ten to twenty early-stage startups, each vetted by CityFund managers for viability. Startups are indexed by city so your investments directly support growth in your community.

Investing for the People

CityFund is changing how people can get involved in startups. No longer must you be a high net-worth accredited investor. For the first time since 1933, you can legally have an ownership stake in the startups you invest in as a non-accredited investor, and with CityFund, you can invest as little as you like in as many or few index funds as you like.
